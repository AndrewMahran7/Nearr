# CO-FOUNDER AGREEMENT

This Cofounder Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on [DATE] between:

- Andrew Mahran (“Founder”)
- Timothy Shahbaz (“Cofounder”)

Together referred to as the “Parties”.

---

# 1. PURPOSE

The Parties agree to collaborate on building and growing Nearr (“the Company”), a mobile application focused on saving, remembering, and acting on real-world places discovered online.

---

# 2. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

## Founder (Andrew Mahran)
- Product development
- Engineering and technical architecture
- Product direction and feature implementation

## Cofounder (Timothy Shahbaz)
- User acquisition
- Growth strategy and execution
- User onboarding and activation
- Retention efforts and feedback collection
- Weekly reporting of metrics and insights

---

# 3. PERFORMANCE PERIOD (60-DAY TRIAL)

The Cofounder will have a **60-day performance period** starting from the date of this Agreement.

During this period, the Cofounder is responsible for driving the Company to **Stage 3 (Habit Loop)** as defined in the Nearr Version Gate System.

This includes successfully achieving all required metrics across:

- Stage 0 (Foundation)
- Stage 1 (Core Loop)
- Stage 2 (Friction Removal)

AND demonstrating progress toward Stage 3 metrics.

---

## Minimum Expectations During Trial

The Cofounder must:

- Actively acquire users weekly
- Ensure users correctly use the product (save → view → act)
- Collect and report user feedback
- Provide weekly updates including:
  - Downloads
  - WAU
  - Saves per user
  - Decision activity (WAD proxy)
  - Key learnings and blockers

---

# 4. OUTCOME OF PERFORMANCE PERIOD

## If Performance Targets Are Met

If the Founder determines, in good faith, that the Cofounder has successfully driven the Company to Stage 3 readiness:

- Cofounder will be granted **30% equity** in the Company
- Equity will be subject to standard vesting (see Section 5)

---

## If Performance Targets Are NOT Met

If the Cofounder does not meet expectations:

- No equity will be granted
- This Agreement may be terminated by either party
- No further obligations between the Parties

---

# 5. VESTING

If equity is granted:

- Vesting period: **4 years**
- Cliff: **1 year**
- After the cliff, equity vests monthly

If the Cofounder leaves before the 1-year cliff:

- 0% equity is retained

---

# 6. DECISION MAKING

- Founder has final authority on product and technical decisions
- Cofounder has primary authority on growth strategy
- In case of disagreement, the Founder has final decision authority

---

# 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work, ideas, code, designs, and intellectual property created in connection with Nearr will be owned entirely by the Company.

Both Parties agree to assign all relevant IP to the Company.

---

# 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both Parties agree to keep all Company information confidential, including:

- Product details
- User data
- Growth strategies
- Technical implementation

---

# 9. TERMINATION

Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time during the 60-day period.

After equity is granted, termination is governed by vesting terms.

---

# 10. GENERAL

This Agreement is intended to be a simple, good-faith understanding between the Parties.

A more formal legal agreement may be created in the future if needed.
